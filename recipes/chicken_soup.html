Instant Pot Chicken Noodle Soup

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Parsley
  • Oregano
  • Thyme
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Carrot
  • Chicken Stock
  • Chicken Stock Cube
  • Water
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Sautee the vegies with the salt, pepper, and chicken stock cube until the onion is translucent
  2. Then add in your parsley, oregano, and thyme
  3. Once fragrant, pour in your stock and your water, add in your chicken
  4. Set the pressure cooker on high for 15 minutes
  5. Once finished, release the pressure, season to taste and enjoy your meal!
