Carbonara

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Bacon
  • Egg Yolk
  • Pepper
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Add the egg yolk to a bowl, grate a whopping amount of parmesan on it and crack some pepper
  2. Whisk together until it makes a sauce like mixture
  3. Meanwhile cook the bacon in a pan until it's suuuper crispy, then drain that sweet sweet bacon fat into the mixture made previously
  4. Once the bacon fat is mixed in, add your cooked pasta and coat it in that flavoursome carbonara sauce
  5. Enjoy you devious dawg
